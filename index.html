What is Train with Soph?

I found happiness through fitness, and created TWS as a platform to positively impact thousands of lives around the world. My goal is to provide all women a supportive environment to build a body and life that they can be proud of!

Like so many, the original goal was simple, I wanted to change my body, build muscle and lose fat.

There were a lot of ups and downs (there still are!), but I created a body and lifestyle that I enjoy and love. I want to share my learnings with you, and help you on your journey too.

Why TWS?

The TWS Platform is designed to provide EVERYTHING you need to make your fitness journey an enjoyable one. I’ve made sure to give you a customised, personal experience that works off your lifestyle and what you need from a program.

Stay accountable, track your progress and join a community of like-minded women on a journey similar to yours! Know that my team and I will be with you, every step of the way.
